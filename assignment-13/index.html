MHS Photography

Photography Based Out of the Rocky Mountains

Welcome

Since 2015, MHS Photography has been your source for capturing moments and light in the Missoula area.

With a passion for capturing the beauty of Earth and its people, I have spent the last five years photographing everything from the mountains to the ocean. My intention is to spread beauty and encourage others to find light in their own lives. I do most of my photography by myself, but sometimes have the assistance of one or two other photographers. I make sure to pay attention to the details and not overlook the little things that give places and people character.

My photography education came from Rocky Mountain School of Photography, a small trade school in the center of Missoula, Montana. My training there was 6 months with at least seven hours of instructed photography a day. I learned to work with different lighting systems, editing practices, and studios. Although my studio experiences have been great, my heart has been set on shooting on location. I believe in meeting people in places where they feel comfortable.

While photography takes up most of my time, I enjoy working with other mediums as well. I believe that painting, drawing, and working with my hands has helped me develope my photography style more than just shooting ever could. My background in the visual arts has taught me how to pay attention to color, composition, and form. As I continue my studies in art, my photography has remained a focus and passion.
