Champagne Cream Sauce Over Lemon Basil Ricotta Stuffed Shells

Ingredients

  • 1 12 oz Box of Jumbo Pasta Shells
  • 1 and a half cup Lemon Basil Ricotta
  • 1 cup Pecorino Romano or Parmesan Cheese (grated)
  • 3 Tablespoons of Butter
  • 1 clove Minced Garlic
  • 3 Tablespoons All-Purpose Flour
  • 3/4 Cup Champagne
  • 1/2 Cup Heavy Cream
  • Kosher Salt and Black Pepper to Taste

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F
  2. Butter large baking dish
  3. Bring large pot of water to boil and season with kosher salt
  4. Cook jumbo shell pasta 2-3 minutes shy of al dente
  5. Season lemon basil ricotta with salt and pepper
  6. Mis half of grated pecorino/parmesan into ricotta
  7. Once shells are ready, drain and stuff with ricotta
  8. Place stuffed shells into buttered baking dish

    9. Sauce

  9. Put small sauce pan on mediam heat and melt 3 tablespoons of butter
  10. Add clove of minced garlic ad cook until soft, but not browned
  11. Whisk in three tablespoons of flur to create roux
  12. Pour in champagne and heavy cream while whisking
  13. Let sauce bubble until thick (usually a few minutes)
  14. Add pepper and pour on stuffed shells
  15. Place into oven and bake until sauce is bubbling and cheese has melted (20-25 minutes)
  16. Add the rest of the pecorino/parmesan before serving
