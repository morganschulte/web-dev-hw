About

I chose to do an instructable on how to teach your dog to crawl because I think this is a good example of how easy it is to train a dog. With so many animals being dropped off by their owners due to owners being overwhelmed, I think its important for people to know that you can get a dog under control.

To learn more about training, check out The Spruce Pets

To get a dog to train, please visit The Western Humane Society
